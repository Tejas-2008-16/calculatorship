Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Compoundly ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at www.compoundly.example. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of our financial calculators and related content.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

All calculator inputs — including monthly investment amounts, expected return rates, investment durations, and target wealth goals — are processed entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. We do not transmit, store, or process any of your financial inputs on our servers. We have no visibility into any numbers you enter into our tools.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we may collect certain standard technical information through our hosting infrastructure and analytics services. This includes:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and session duration
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)

This data is used in aggregate form only to understand traffic patterns and improve our site's performance. It is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

3. Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies to enhance user experience. Specifically, we store your preferred display theme (dark or light mode) in your browser's localStorage. This preference never leaves your device. For full details about our cookie usage, please read our Cookie Policy.

4. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We participate in the Google AdSense program to display contextually relevant advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your browsing history and interests. We do not control Google's data practices. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they process data for advertising purposes.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Newsletter Subscription

If you voluntarily submit your email address through our newsletter signup form, we store that address solely for the purpose of sending you our financial education newsletter. We do not sell or share your email address with third parties. You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

6. Contact Form Submissions

When you submit our contact form, the information you provide (name, email, subject, and message) is used only to respond to your specific inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without explicit consent.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect our website infrastructure. However, since we do not collect or store your financial inputs, there is no sensitive financial data at risk on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be indicated by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: Contact Page or email us at privacy@compoundly.example.
