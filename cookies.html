Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Calculatorship uses cookies and similar local storage technologies when you visit our website at calculatorship.in.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a more personalized experience. Local Storage is a similar technology that allows websites to store data directly in your browser, which persists between sessions.

2. How We Use Cookies & Local Storage

We keep our cookie usage minimal and privacy-respecting. Here is exactly what we store:

Essential Functional Storage

We use browser localStorage (not a traditional cookie) to remember your chosen display theme (dark mode or light mode). This ensures a consistent visual experience across page visits. The data stored is only the string "dark" or "light" under the key Calculatorship-theme. It contains no personal information and never leaves your device.

Third-Party Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

We participate in the Google AdSense advertising program. Google places cookies on your device to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing interests and history across sites. These cookies are controlled by Google, not by Calculatorship. The primary cookies Google may set include:

  • _gads — Google Ads interest categories
  • IDE — Used by Google DoubleClick to register and report user actions after viewing or clicking an advertisement
  • DSID — Used to track conversions for Google Ads campaigns
  • NID — Registers a unique ID for Google preferences

You can learn more about Google's advertising cookies at Google Cookie Policy.

Analytics (If Applicable)

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymized, aggregate traffic data. If active, Analytics cookies include _ga and _gid, which store an anonymous client ID to distinguish visitors. No personally identifiable information is linked to these identifiers.

3. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here are links to cookie management instructions for major browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and may prevent Google from serving relevant advertisements.

4. Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of Google's personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You may also visit aboutads.info for industry-wide opt-out options.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable regulations. Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
